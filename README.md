## About Me

I am interested in computational methods for analyzing immune-cell
heterogeneity, clonal dynamics, and spatial organization.

My current research focuses on:

- Integration of scRNA-seq with scTCR-seq and scBCR-seq
- B- and T-cell clonal expansion and differentiation
- Spatial transcriptomics and spatial immune repertoires
- Gene-regulatory networks and cellular-state dynamics
- Machine learning for single-cell multi-omics

## Research Interests

### Single-cell immune repertoire analysis

Developing computational frameworks that jointly model transcriptional
states and immune-receptor information.

### Spatial immunology

Transferring clonotype-associated transcriptional programs from paired
single-cell RNA and immune-repertoire data to spatial transcriptomics.

### Cellular dynamics

Studying differentiation, clonal expansion, class-switch recombination,
and gene-regulatory-network transitions.
